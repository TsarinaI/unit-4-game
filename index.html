Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. The crystals will each have a different number assigned to them. By clicking on the crystals below, you will add value to your total score. You win by reaching the exact total of the random number. You lose if you go over the exact total of the random number. Everytime you win, lose, or reload the page, the crystals are given a new value. Good luck!
